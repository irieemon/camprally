**Formula A: Pain Point Hook**

"Spent [X nights/days] testing [product]. Here's what happened." → details → CTA
- Best for: Reels, single-product deep dives

**Formula C: List Hook**

- Best for: Carousels, educational content

- Best for: engagement, shares, comments
